  "description": "A careful history and focused neurologic examination is usually adequate to exclude secondary headache. It is important to elicit red flags that are mostly secondary causes of headache.",
  "red_flags": [
    "Systemic symptoms: fever, chills, myalgia, weight loss (Metastasis, infection)",
    "Neurological symptoms or deficits (Stroke, mass lesion, encephalitis)",
    "Older age at onset (> 50 years) (Temporal arteritis, glaucoma, mass lesion)",
    "Thunderclap headache onset (Bleed)",
    "Papilloedema (Raised intracranial pressure)",
    "Positional (Intracranial hypotension)",
    "Precipitated by Valsalva manoeuvre or exertion (Raised intracranial pressure)",
    "Progressive headache or substantial pattern change (Any secondary cause)"
