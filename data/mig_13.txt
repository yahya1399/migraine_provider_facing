The presence of four out of five criteria in the POUND mnemonic gives a likelihood ratio of 24 for possible migraine.(13) POUND is a user-friendly screening tools that encompass the major criteria in the ICHD-3 diagnostic criteria for migraine.

POUND Screening Tool (1 point for each symptom).
P Pulsating
O Duration of 4–72 hours
U Unilateral
N Nausea
D Disabling

Likelihood ratio for definite or possible migraine:
• 4 points: 24
• 3 points: 3.5
• < 2 points: 0.41
